Tims Hockey Challenge – Full Strategy Breakdown

Game Structure

  • 3 picks per day from tiered probability buckets
  • Rewards: 25 / 50 / 100 points for 1 / 2 / 3 correct picks
  • 7-day streak requires ≥1 hit daily
  • Leaderboard rewards favor high-variance outcomes

Core Insight

The scoring system is convex, meaning 3-hit outcomes are disproportionately valuable. This makes correlation between picks critical.

Thresholds

  • Streak: 10–15% drop max
  • EV: 20–25%
  • Leaderboard: 30–40%

Edge Cases

  • 2 games: 2 independent + 1 same team
  • 1 game: use opposing teams for streaks

Key Takeaways

  • Correlation is the main edge
  • Pick 3 is the most important
  • Avoid opposing unless forced
