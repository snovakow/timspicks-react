Tim Hortons NHL Hockey Challenge Strategy

Core Insight

The challenge is a streak survival game, not a perfect-pick game.

You only need at least one correct pick out of three each day.

Objective

Maximize:

P(at least 1 correct) = 1 - P(all 3 miss)

Strategy Comparison

Same Team (Stacking)

  • Positive correlation
  • Higher chance all fail together
  • Bad for streaks

Different Teams (Independent)

  • Low correlation
  • Lower chance all fail together
  • Best for streak survival

Opposing Teams

  • Moderate correlation
  • Middle-ground performance

Ranking

  1. Different games/teams (Best)
  2. Opposing teams
  3. Same team (Worst)

Final Takeaway

Diversification beats stacking.

Pick players from different games to minimize the risk of all three failing.
