Tim Hortons Hockey Challenge Strategy Guide

Key Objectives

  • Streaks: Get at least 1 of 3 picks correct daily
  • Leaderboard: Maximize total correct picks over time

Correlation Effects

  • Same Team: Positive correlation (boom or bust)
  • Opposing Teams: Negative correlation (safer for streaks)
  • Different Games: Near independence

Best Strategy for Streaks

Minimize the chance that all 3 picks fail:
  • Best: Opposing teams
  • Good: Different games
  • Worst: Same team stacking

Best Strategy for Total Picks

Maximize expected value (EV):
  • Pick highest probability players
  • Ignore correlation
  • Stacking teams is fine

Key Formula

Expected Picks Per Day:

E = p₁ + p₂ + p₃

Pro Strategy

  • Use sportsbook odds → convert to probabilities
  • De-vig across books for accuracy
  • Pick highest EV players in each set
  • Use correlation only as a tie-breaker

Final Takeaways

  • Streaks → diversify picks
  • Total picks → maximize probability
  • Hybrid → balance EV and risk
